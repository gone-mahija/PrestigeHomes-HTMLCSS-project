Founded in 2008, Prestige Homes was built on a simple principle: every client deserves exceptional service and access to the finest properties available.

What started as a boutique agency has grown into one of the region's most respected luxury real estate firms, with over $2 billion in sales and hundreds of satisfied clients.

Our team of experienced agents brings deep market knowledge, strong negotiation skills, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. Whether you're buying your first home or selling a multi-million dollar estate, we provide the same level of dedicated, personalized service.
